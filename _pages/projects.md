<ul>

	<li>
		<b>Evaluating Gender Bias in Natural Language Inference</b>
		<br>
		<ul>
		<li>Proposed an evaluation methodology by constructing a challenge task to demonstrate that gender bias is exhibited in state-of-the-art Fine-tuned Transformer based NLI model outputs.</li>
		<li>Tested augmentation as an existing de-biasing technique and understood its efficacy on various state-of-the-art NLI Models. </li>
		<li>Paper accepted at <a href="https://securedata.lol/">NeurIPS 2020 Workshop on Dataset Curation and Security.</a></li>
		</ul>

	</li><br>

	<li>
		<b>Detecting Depression Triggers in YouTube videos</b>
		<br>
		<ul>
		<li>Used Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks to understand the video content from transcripts of YouTube videos and identified if a video can trigger depression with an accuracy of 83%.</li>
		<li>Additionally, proposed a novel method for validating the results by analysing the CES-D score of comments.</li>
		<li>Paper selected at <a href="https://aiforsocialgood.github.io/neurips2019/">NeurIPS 2019 AI4SG Workshop.</a></li>
		</ul>
		
	</li><br>

	<li>
		<b>COVID-19 'People - Resource Provider' Mapping App</b>
		<br>
		<ul>
		<li>Developed an application to make it possible for people-in-need in times like pandemics to reach out for help by just making a phone call or sending an SMS without having access to internet.</li>
		<li>Developed and built algorithms that can automatically suggest and match people-in-need to the best resource providers (NGOs, Volunteers).</li>
		<li>Developed as part of hack:now, a global hackathon for a non-profit "DaanMatch". <a href="https://github.com/manandey/COVID-19-People-Resource-Provider-Mapping-App/blob/master/README.md">GitHub</a></li>
		</ul>
	</li><br>
