	<li>
		<b>Evaluating Gender Bias in Natural Language Inference</b>
		<br>
		<ul>
		<li>Proposed an evaluation methodology by constructing a challenge task to demonstrate that gender bias is exhibited in state-of-the-art fine-tuned Transformer based NLI model outputs.</li>
		<li>Tested augmentation as an existing de-biasing technique and understand its efficacy on various state-of-the-art NLI Models. <i>[Paper selected at <a href="https://securedata.lol/">Workshop on Dataset Curation and Security - NeurIPS 2020.</a>]</i></li>
		</ul>
		
	</li><br>

	<li>
		<b>Detecting Depression Triggers in YouTube videos</b>
		<br>
		<ul>
		<li>Used Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks to understand the video content form the transcripts to identify if a video can trigger depression or not with an accuracy of 83%.</li>
		<li>Additionally, we propose a novel method of validating the results by analysing the CESD score of comments.<i>[Paper selected at <a href="https://aiforsocialgood.github.io/neurips2019/">NeurIPS 2019 AI4SG Workshop.</a>]</i></li>
		</ul>
		
	</li><br>
