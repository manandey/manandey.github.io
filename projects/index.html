projects

  • Evaluating Gender Bias in Natural Language Inference
    • Proposed an evaluation methodology by constructing a challenge task to demonstrate that gender bias is exhibited in state-of-the-art Fine-tuned Transformer based NLI model outputs.
    • Tested augmentation as an existing de-biasing technique and understood its efficacy on various state-of-the-art NLI Models.
    • Paper accepted at NeurIPS 2020 Workshop on Dataset Curation and Security.

  • Detecting Depression Triggers in YouTube videos
    • Used Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks to understand the video content from transcripts of YouTube videos and identified if a video can trigger depression with an accuracy of 83%.
    • Additionally, proposed a novel method for validating the results by analysing the CES-D score of comments.
    • Paper selected at NeurIPS 2019 AI4SG Workshop.

  • COVID-19 'People - Resource Provider' Mapping App
    • Developed an application to make it possible for people-in-need in times like pandemics to reach out for help by just making a phone call or sending an SMS without having access to internet.
    • Developed and built algorithms that can automatically suggest and match people-in-need to the best resource providers (NGOs, Volunteers).
    • Developed as part of hack:now, a global hackathon for a non-profit "DaanMatch". GitHub

  • Store-based clustering problem
    • Worked on a store-based clustering problem to build relevant store segments which are homogenous in certain behavioural aspects and can be targeted using the same marketing strategy. PCA and k-means algorithm were used for this problem.
    • Internship project at Impact Analytics, Bangalore

  • Analysis of Clustering Algorithms on Isolated word data.
    • Studied, deployed and analysed various Clustering Algorithms on Isolated Word Data and measured their performance based on some extrinsic metrics namely Pair Counting measures, B-Cubed Measures and Set-Matching based measures.
    • Internship project at Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati (IITG)
